4 — About the Marksheet Preview

The View Marksheet button loads the student's full Class IX report card with all seeded marks (both half-yearly and final-term placeholders). This is a pre-lock preview — co-scholastic grades, attendance, remarks, and rank can be filled by the class teacher (SFST004) before locking. After locking, the marksheet becomes the official record.
